SPF Certification Program

Caliber Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) certification program prepares the installers for employment in the Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) trade. Basic theory and related information along with hands-on practical application enable installers to become competent in basic Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) applications and trouble shooting.

Upon successful completion of the program, installers can seek employment as certified installers. The Canadian Construction Materials Centre (CCMC) has approved this program as meeting the technical training requirements of the building code and current standard CAN/ULC 705.2. Canada Construction Material Centre

Approved, Trusted Nationwide

The CCMC publishes product evaluations for spray foam products and provides corresponding evaluation numbers. These reports and numbers provide building officials across Canada with confidence that the spray foam products meet the requirements outlined in the building code. To help ensure those spray foam products are properly installed, the reports requires supplies to have a field quality assurance program administered by a third-party provider. Caliber is recognized by CCMC as a provider. The pillars of a quality assurance program include installer training and demonstrated competence, monitoring of field installation and implementation of a framework that leads to consistent quality outcomes. In that regard, installer training and certification is a critical part in maintaining the confidence of all stakeholders through high-quality installations.

ISO Accredited Certification Body

The SPF installer certification is accredited for conformance to International Standard ISO/IEC 17024 – Conformity Assessment – General Requirements for Bodies Operating Certification of Persons. This means that the certification and issuing body Caliber comply with internally agreed upon best practices. and high standards. This brings credibility and recognition of this certification to other stakeholders in the construction industry.
